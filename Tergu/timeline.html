The timeline of Tergu

As you can see, this is a brief timeline of Tergu

The Sultimathischnorisis eon (About 7 billion to 4.15 billion years ago) Subdivided into 4 Tubungveras (eras) Kimivichen ( From 7-5.34 billion years ago) Subdivided into 2 Premiveras (periods) Orizhinik ( From 7 billion - 6.2 billion years ago) Strivichinik (From 6.2 - 5.34 billion years ago): The first period of micro-biological species. Alikhopen ( From 5.34 - 4.92 billion years ago) Blavaleshtien ( From 4.92 - 4.21 billion years ago) Ondinungstarzen ( From 4.21 - 4.15 billion years ago): This era was brought to an abrupt end by the collision of three giant meteorites with Tergu, which caused significant impacts on the ecosystem and the planetary surface, leading to mass extinction of numerous species. The Reskoverikasis eon (From 4.15-2.8 billion years ago) (aka The recovery eon) Subdivided into 3 Tubungveras (eras) Prè-Reskoveriken (From 4.15-4 billion years ago) Prosoreskoveriken (From 4 - 3.5 billion years ago) Manereskoveriken (From 3.5-2.8 billion years ago): ended due to plate divergence. The scale of this tectonic divergence was precipitated by radical thermal shifts within Tergu’s core. The Kelephorosis eon (From 2.8 billion to 30 million years ago) Subdivided into 5 Tubungveras: Balestien (From 2.8 billion - 1.98 billion years ago) Balestatien (From 1.98 - 1.2 billion years ago) Mongerustiyen (from 1.2 billion to 900 million years ago) Groronzhen (From 900 - 499 million years ago) Magorovichliniyen (From 499 - 30 million years ago): The formation of a new supercontinent followed by the most significant climate change ever. The “Stedetimizhe” or the Ice Age IV marked the end of the third eon on Tergu. The Concugentasis eon (The current eon) Currently, there is only one Tubungvera: Allegor-Tumbastimen (From 30 million years ago - IIII) (IIII means the present.)
